Break-Bulk

In addition to the sea transportation of containerised cargoes Fourway Logistics Solutions offers a service which caters for conventional (break bulk) cargo. Our service providers operate several Multi Purpose services around the globe. The Multi Purpose service is capable of carrying both conventional (break-bulk) as well as containerised cargo. As niche operators we are in a position to react fast and are often able to accommodate cargo at short notice. With our strategic partners, we are also flexible in terms of ports of call and where possible we will call at a alternative port should your cargo be too large to be accommodated by one of our regular ports of call.
